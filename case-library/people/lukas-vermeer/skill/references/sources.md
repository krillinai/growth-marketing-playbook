## Evidence Rules

- Attribute claims to the specific speaker and setting.
- Treat Booking.com and Vista descriptions as practitioner accounts, not controlled evaluations of the companies.
- Do not infer internal metrics, experiment outcomes, interaction prevalence, or current practices.
- Separate statistical method from organizational recommendation.
- State when a conclusion depends on traffic, interference, metric quality, or platform design.
